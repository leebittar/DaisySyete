01

Provide Your Information

Fill out a simple form with your basic details including **client type, age, region of residence, and the service you utilize**. This helps us understand who we are serving and which departments to evaluate. The entire process is designed to be quick and straightforward, taking only a few minutes to complete. All information is kept strictly confidential and will be used solely for improving our services.

02

Answer Awareness Questions

Tell us about your **knowledge of the Citizen’s Charter and the services offered** by the office you transacted with. This includes questions on whether the Citizen’s Charter was visible, understandable, and adhered to. This helps us improve our communication, process transparency efforts, and public awareness programs regarding our services.

03

Rate Your Experience

Evaluate the quality of service you received using our **satisfaction rating scale**. Rate various aspects from **staff courtesy, facility cleanliness, processing time, and overall service quality**. Your ratings help us identify specific areas for improvement and ensure that service delivery standards are consistently met across all departments.

04

Share Additional Feedback

This step gives you an opportunity to **comment on nearly every section** of the survey, allowing you to provide specific suggestions for improvement or commendation. This free-text feedback is highly valuable, as it captures the nuances and specific challenges faced during your transaction. Once completed, simply click the **"Submit"** button. You'll receive an instant confirmation, and your valuable insights will be routed to the relevant department levels and used to fuel policy and service enhancements.
